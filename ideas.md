<idea>
  **Design Movement**: Cyberpunk / Neon Noir
  **Core Principles**:
  1. **High Contrast**: Deep blacks against vibrant neons (purple, pink, yellow) to create a "night mode" feel that fits the "degen" crypto aesthetic.
  2. **Glitch & Distortion**: Subtle glitch effects on hover and transitions to emphasize the digital/web3 nature.
  3. **Gamification First**: UI elements should look like game HUDs (Heads-Up Displays) rather than standard web components.
  4. **Transparency & Glassmorphism**: Use frosted glass effects to layer content over dynamic backgrounds.

  **Color Philosophy**:
  - **Background**: Deep void black (#050505) to midnight purple (#1a0b2e).
  - **Primary**: Electric Purple (#9333ea) and Hot Pink (#db2777) for main actions.
  - **Accent**: Gold/Yellow (#eab308) for winning/money-related elements (crowns, coins).
  - **Intent**: Evoke excitement, risk, and reward. The dark theme reduces eye strain for late-night trading/gaming sessions.

  **Layout Paradigm**:
  - **Asymmetric Grid**: Break the standard 12-column grid with overlapping elements and floating containers.
  - **HUD-style Navigation**: Fixed side or bottom navigation that resembles a game inventory or status bar.
  - **Card-based Content**: Content lives in "modules" or "cards" with glowing borders.

  **Signature Elements**:
  - **Neon Borders**: Glowing borders on active elements.
  - **Pixel Art Icons**: Mix of vector icons and pixel art for a retro-future vibe.
  - **Floating Particles**: Background canvas with floating particles (confetti/coins) as seen in the original code.

  **Interaction Philosophy**:
  - **Instant Feedback**: Every click should have a sound or visual ripple.
  - **Micro-interactions**: Buttons scale down on click; cards lift on hover.
  - **Live Updates**: Real-time counters for points/spins without page reloads.

  **Animation**:
  - **Entrance**: Elements slide in with a "glitch" effect or fade up with a slight bounce.
  - **Hover**: Glow intensity increases; text might shift slightly.
  - **Success**: Explosion of confetti (already in code) and screen shake for big wins.

  **Typography System**:
  - **Headings**: 'Orbitron' or 'Press Start 2P' (Google Fonts) for a tech/gaming feel.
  - **Body**: 'Rajdhani' or 'Chakra Petch' for readable but stylized text.
  - **Hierarchy**: Uppercase, bold headings with spaced-out tracking.
</idea>

<idea>
  **Design Movement**: Royal Luxury / Baroque Digital
  **Core Principles**:
  1. **Opulence**: Use gold gradients, velvet textures, and ornate borders to emphasize the "King" aspect.
  2. **Skeuomorphism Lite**: Buttons that look like physical gems or gold bars.
  3. **Centralized Focus**: The "Throne" (main content) is always center stage, framed by curtains or pillars.
  4. **Smooth & Heavy**: Animations feel heavy and substantial, like moving gold bars.

  **Color Philosophy**:
  - **Background**: Rich Royal Blue (#0f172a) or Velvet Red (#450a0a).
  - **Primary**: Gold Gradients (Linear gradient from #fcd34d to #b45309).
  - **Accent**: Emerald Green (#10b981) for success/money.
  - **Intent**: Make the user feel like a wealthy ruler. High-value aesthetic.

  **Layout Paradigm**:
  - **Symmetrical/Centered**: Classic, stable layout representing order and power.
  - **Frame-within-Frame**: Content areas have decorative borders (filigree).

  **Signature Elements**:
  - **Crown Motifs**: Used as dividers, icons, and background patterns.
  - **Serif Typography**: Elegant headings.
  - **Shine Effects**: Periodic "shine" animations on gold elements.

  **Interaction Philosophy**:
  - **Deliberate**: Interactions are slower but smoother.
  - **Tactile**: Buttons depress deeply.

  **Animation**:
  - **Parallax**: Background layers move slower than foreground.
  - **Fade & Scale**: Elegant transitions.

  **Typography System**:
  - **Headings**: 'Cinzel' or 'Playfair Display' for royal elegance.
  - **Body**: 'Lato' or 'Montserrat' for clean readability.
</idea>

  **Design Movement**: Solarpunk / Eco-Futurism
  **Core Principles**:
  1. **Organic Shapes**: Rounded corners, fluid blobs, and natural curves.
  2. **Bright & Airy**: Light backgrounds with vibrant, nature-inspired colors.
  3. **Transparency**: Glassmorphism with blur to create depth.
  4. **Optimism**: Visuals that suggest growth, abundance, and a positive future.

  **Color Philosophy**:
  - **Background**: Soft Cream (#fffbeb) or Pale Blue (#f0f9ff).
  - **Primary**: Vibrant Green (#22c55e) and Sky Blue (#0ea5e9).
  - **Accent**: Solar Yellow (#facc15).
  - **Intent**: A fresh, clean look that stands out from the typical "dark mode" crypto sites.

  **Layout Paradigm**:
  - **Fluid/Masonry**: Content flows naturally without rigid grids.
  - **Floating Elements**: UI components float above the background with soft shadows.

  **Signature Elements**:
  - **Leaf/Sun Icons**: Nature-inspired iconography.
  - **Soft Gradients**: Mesh gradients in the background.

  **Interaction Philosophy**:
  - **Bouncy**: Spring physics for animations.
  - **Fluid**: Elements morph into each other.

  **Animation**:
  - **Morphing**: Shapes change form.
  - **Floating**: Idle animations for background elements.

  **Typography System**:
  - **Headings**: 'Quicksand' or 'Nunito' for rounded, friendly vibes.
  - **Body**: 'DM Sans' for modern clarity.
